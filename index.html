About Me

I am a Professional Engineer (PE) currently living in Winter Garden, Florida, United States. I obtained a bachelor's degree in Electrical/Computer Engineering from the University of Puerto Rico in 1993, and a Master's Degree in Software Engineering from the Polytechnical University of Puerto Rico in 2010. I am an Oracle Certified Professional (OCP) - DBA Track - since version 7.3, circa 1998. During my professional life, I have worked as a "C/C++" Language programmer, Oracle Database Administrator, Unix system administrtor, Technical Sales Consultant, Oracle Applications Support Engineer and Implementation Consultant. I moved to Orlando Florida in 2013 from Puerto Rico to work with Oracle Corporation as a Senior Principal Support Engineer in the Tier-3 DBA Support Team (Part of Oracle Advanced Support Services), a role I still work at to this day. My interests are: anything that has to do with computer hardware and software design, and electronics design in general. My hobbies are : Cross-fit, Running, biking, car repair, guitar playing and just spending time with my family. I am a big fan of the "Ghost Adventures", TV program from the Travel Channel.
